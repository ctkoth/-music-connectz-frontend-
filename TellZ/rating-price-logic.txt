TellZ: StatZ Rating Price Logic

- Final Price = Base Price × (1 + (Median Rating of Content − 5) × 0.1)
    - If median rating is 5, price stays the same.
    - If median rating is 4, price is -10%.
    - If median rating is 3, price is -20%.
    - If median rating is 6, price is +10%.
- This formula rewards higher-rated content and discounts lower-rated content, using the median rating for fairness.
- All price adjustments are logged for transparency and tracked as TellZ.
